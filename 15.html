ABOUT US......

Established in 1985, Dr. T.M.A. Pai Polytechnic Manipal has developed into one of the best polytechnic colleges in India. Part of the renowned academic township of Manipal, Dr. T.M.A. Pai polytechnic is closely associated with some of the most reputed colleges in the country. We are approved and recognised by the All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi (AICTE) and affiliated to the Board of Technical Education, Bangalore. Situated on a large, independent campus, we have been catering to the local population of Udupi and Dakshina Kannada districts as well as students from other parts of the state and country.Tonse Madhava Ananth Pai (30 April 1898 – 29 May 1979), was an Indian physician, educationist, banker and philanthropist, most well-known for building the university town of Manipal, India. He was the first to start a private, self-financing medical college offering MBBS in India. Pai established the Kasturba Medical College in 1953 and Manipal Institute of Technology in 1957, which was followed by a string of other education institutions including Manipal College of Dental Sciences, Manipal College of Pharmaceutical Sciences and Manipal Pre-University College. He, along with his brother Upendra Ananth Pai, also established Syndicate Bank originally in Udupi, Karnataka, which has its headquarters now in Manipal and Bangalore. He was responsible for its popular Pigmy Deposit Scheme.d 21 July 2015.
